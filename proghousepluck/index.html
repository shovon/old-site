Progressive House Pluck for ZynAddSubFX

Love the sound used in most house and progressive house songs, such as Move For Me by Deadmau5 & Kaskade, Amazing by Inna, Stereo Love by Edward Maya? Want to use that sound on your own tracks?

Now, you can. Just click the big download button, below.

Progressive House Pluck Demo

The above are example chord progressions played using the progressive house pluck.

If you really like how all of this sounds, and you want your tracks to sound like this, then click on the download button, and start using it on your own tracks.

Download

If you really like the sound a lot, how 'bout buying me coffee? :)

Please donate. :)

When you download, unzip the file progressive-house-pluck.xpf over to your LMMS' presets folder, which is found in

  • home > your_username > lmms > presets on Linux, and
  • Users > your_username > lmms > presets on Windows.

And don't worry, you are free to use it anywhere. :)
